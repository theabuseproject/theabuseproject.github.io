The Abuse Project — Project TAP

Eradicating audio profanity from the Internet

This all was started with a silly project idea that was initially found on some reddit thread which involved generating abuse words. Long story short, while sitting with one of my friends, he told me about how time consuming it is to do repetitive tasks, like, "muting or beeping abusive words from a survey video and then re-render the clean video" and that was the "aha!" moment which kick-started Project TAP — The Abuse Project.
