Our Story

This all was started with a silly project idea that was initially found on some reddit thread which involved generating abuse words. Long story short, while sitting with my friend, Manav Kapil, he was telling me about how time consuming it is to do repetitive tasks, like, "muting or beeping abusive words from a survey video, re-rendering it" and that was the moment which kick-started this project.
